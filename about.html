About Indian Classical Dance


Indian classical dance is one of the oldest and most respected performing arts in India. These dance forms combine graceful movements, facial expressions, hand gestures (mudras), music, and storytelling to express emotions and cultural traditions.


There are eight officially recognized classical dance forms in India. Each dance form has its own unique style, costumes, music, and historical significance.


This website aims to introduce visitors to some of India's most famous classical dance forms and encourage appreciation for India's rich cultural heritage.
