Indian Classical Dance Forms

Explore some of the most famous classical dance forms of India.

Bharatanatyam

Bharatanatyam originated in Tamil Nadu and is one of the oldest classical dance forms in India. It is known for graceful movements, expressions, and intricate footwork.

Kathak

Kathak originated in North India. It is famous for storytelling, rhythmic footwork, elegant spins, and expressive gestures.

Kuchipudi

Kuchipudi is a classical dance form from Andhra Pradesh. It combines dance, music, and drama to narrate stories.

Kathakali

Kathakali originated in Kerala. It is recognized for colorful costumes, elaborate makeup, and dramatic facial expressions.

Odissi

Odissi comes from Odisha and is one of India's oldest dance forms. It is known for graceful poses and devotional themes.
